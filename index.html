Welcome

If you feel you may have a gambling problem please call the hotline, attend a meeting, or email us at ocigchair@gmail.com and get started on the road to recovery now.

We are a nonprofit, self-supporting fellowship of recovering compulsive gamblers who meet regularly to help each other recover from the disease of compulsive gambling. Our guides are the 12 Steps of Recovery and the 12 Steps of Unity of Gamblers Anonymous. The only requirement for membership is a desire to stop gambling.

Need Help? Call the Orange County Hotline:

Orange County Gamblers Anonymous welcomes everyone regardless of age, gender, race, spiritual belief or sexual orientation.

The sole purpose of this website is to aid in the dissemination of recovery related information to local members and visitors from the Web, whether they are members of Gamblers Anonymous or not.
